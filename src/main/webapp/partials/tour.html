Inspire one another to do good.

Karma Exchange not only makes it easy to search and sign up for volunteer events, but it also automatically tracks your impact and enables you to set monthly volunteering goals. Also, you can help your friends gain some karma by inviting them to an upcoming event. Join us, and be part of a community committed to giving back!

Set monthly karma goals

Set a monthly karma goal and view your friends’ karma goals. Encourage each other to earn karma!

Search and register for events

Look for events that interest you. Search by location, time, activity type, or by events your friends are attending. Take a look at the detailed event description and event comments to find out additional information.

Encourage your friends to volunteer

Once you register for an event share it on facebook, twitter, or google+. It’s definitely good karma to help others earn karma. :)

View your impact and others impact

You can see the events you’ve attended and the impact you’ve made in the impact tab on your profile page. Check out your friends profile pages and organization profile pages and be inspired by the difference that you and others are making in your community.
